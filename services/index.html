Services

Data Science Consulting

Our philosophy informs the services we provide, which include:

Advising on data collection and storage to optimize accessibility and usability

Identifying ways in which data can be used to further your mission

Conducting data analysis projects from inception to final product

After dedicating time to understand the problem you'd like to solve, we will work closely with you to:

  • Refine the question(s) to ask to solve the problem
  • Identify the best methods to guard against erroneous conclusions
  • Manage, clean, munge, and merge the data to develop an analyzable data set
  • Apply appropriate data analysis approaches
  • Interpret the results
  • Prepare a final report that describes results using readily accessible language and offers practical solutions to the problem

Training

Training individuals and small groups in scientifically grounded data analysis, using R or Stata.
